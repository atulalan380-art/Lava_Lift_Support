Lava Lift: Logic Rescue – Help & Support

Welcome to the official support page for Lava Lift: Logic Rescue, a hyper-casual lava rescue puzzle inspired by classic river-crossing logic challenges. You pilot a hot-air balloon to transport survivors from the Left Bank (Danger) to the Right Bank (Safety) while avoiding incompatible groups and outrunning rising lava.

About Lava Lift: Logic Rescue

A quick overview of what the game does.

Rescue + Logic Puzzle

Your mission is to ferry survivors across a lava chasm using a balloon with limited capacity. Each level is a logic puzzle: some characters fight, some eat others, some infect, and some protect. The correct solution is the safest crossing sequence.

Rising Lava (Timer)

The lava rises continuously. If it reaches the ledge height, it's game over. Higher levels introduce hazards like wind gusts, falling rocks, geysers, and flying enemies.

Fast Touch Controls

Tap to load/unload characters. Swipe/hold to fly left and right. The game is designed for quick, readable decisions with instant feedback.

Note: Some features may vary depending on the platform and version of the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

How do I start a level?

Tap survivors on the current bank to load them into the balloon (within capacity), then swipe to fly to the other side. Tap survivors in the basket to unload them.

Why did I fail even though the balloon landed safely?

The game fails if an invalid group is left alone on either bank (for example: Wolf + Sheep with no protector), or if lava reaches the cliff ledge height. Most logic fails happen when you fly away from a bank.

Will I lose progress if I reinstall?

This depends on your platform and whether cloud backup/sync is enabled. If you are unsure, contact support with your device details.

The game feels too hard. Any tips?

Try planning one move ahead. Your goal is to keep both banks in a valid state after every crossing. If your build includes hints/rewind, use them to recover from mis-taps.

Troubleshooting

Before contacting support, try these quick checks.

App Not Opening or Crashing

  • Restart your device and try again.
  • Ensure you are on the latest OS version supported by your device.
  • Update Lava Lift: Logic Rescue to the latest version from your app store.
  • If the issue persists, capture a screenshot or screen recording and send it to support.

Touch Input Not Registering

  • Close other heavy apps running in the background.
  • Disable battery saver modes that may restrict performance.
  • Try playing in a cooler environment if your device is overheating.

Performance Issues (Lag / Stutter)

  • Close background apps.
  • Ensure you have enough free storage space on your device.
  • Disable battery saver modes that may throttle GPU/CPU.
